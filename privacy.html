Privacy Policy

Financial Calculator • Last Updated: 05 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the Financial Calculator mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

We value your privacy deeply. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). However, to improve your experience, we do track generic, non-personal usage behavior as detailed below.

  • Financial Data: All calculations, including loan amounts, interest rates, and tenure details entered into the app, are processed completely locally on your device. This data is never sent to our servers or any third parties.
  • Permissions: The App may request access to your device's "Downloads" folder to save generated PDF amortization schedules. This is the sole purpose for which storage-related APIs are used.
  • Usage & Analytics Data: We automatically collect certain non-personally identifiable information through third-party services when you interact with the App to monitor and analyze app stability and feature usage.

2. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes third-party developer tools to help us measure application performance and understand how users interact with our features. Specifically, we use:

  • Google Firebase (Firebase Analytics): This service helps us collect anonymized analytics data. This may include information such as your device type, operating system version, country, session duration, and interactions with buttons or pages inside the App. This data does not contain financial formulas, numbers you calculate, or identity details, and it is strictly used to enhance the App's performance and fix bugs.

For more information on how Google Firebase protects and manages data, please visit the official Google Privacy policy page: Google Privacy & Terms.

3. Data Safety & Security

Your financial metrics and calculation inputs are entirely secure and risk-free from external data breaches because they never leave your device. The non-personal analytics data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit and used purely for statistical optimization.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective immediately upon being posted here.
